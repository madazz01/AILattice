## First comment (post this yourself immediately after launch)
Hey PH! Builder here.

The problem this solves: AI models like ChatGPT and Perplexity are becoming the new search engine, but most websites were never designed for AI to read. Google's model (PageRank, backlinks, visual design) is irrelevant to how AI navigates the web.

AILattice is two things:

**1. An open standard (MIT)** — 4 files you add to any website:
- `/llms.txt` — your AI business card
- `/ai/index.md` — full overview written for AI, not humans
- `/ai/sitemap.md` — map of all AI-navigable pages
- `/ai/*.md` — topic pages (products, pricing, about, etc.)

**2. A registry + search engine** — once you implement the standard and score 70+, you get listed. Organic ranking only. No paid placement ever.

There's an open API anyone can query:
https://ailattice.io/api/search?q=your+topic+here

And it's wired into Claude as an MCP tool natively.

Check your site's AI-readiness score: https://ailattice.io/validate

Happy to answer any questions — especially interested in feedback from people building AI agents.
